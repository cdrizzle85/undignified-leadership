The Journey

Four sessions, carefully sequenced. Each one builds on the last. Your team doesn't just learn — they experience a real transformation together.

Connect → Play → Attune → Align
01
Connect

Personal Connection

We start where trust actually begins: with people knowing each other as people. Your team shares, laughs, and discovers who they're really working alongside. Some will share things no one else in the room knew. That's where real foundation gets built.

  • Deeper knowledge of teammates beyond job titles
  • Early foundation of psychological safety
  • Shared laughter and genuine moments
  • Buy-in and momentum for the sessions ahead
02
Play

Improv Workshop

Using applied improv games and exercises, your team learns to let their guard down. Together. No talent required. Just a willingness to show up. With 20 years of improv experience, this session is designed to be genuinely fun — and no one is ever pressured to participate in anything they don't want to.

  • Shed pretense and self-protection
  • Build spontaneous trust through shared risk
  • Practice the "yes, and" mindset
  • Laugh together. It matters more than it sounds.
03
Attune

Temperaments Training

Your team takes a temperaments assessment and learns how each person is wired: how they think, communicate, and process. Then they learn to use that knowledge to communicate better. The result is less friction, more empathy, and a team that actually understands each other.

  • Understand your own communication style
  • See teammates through a new lens of empathy
  • Learn to shape your words to uplift and clarify
  • Reduce friction from misread intentions
04
Align

Working Genius

Your team takes the Working Genius assessment and discovers where each person naturally thrives and where they drain. Together, you map your team's strengths and identify how to work with the grain, not against it. You leave with a shared language for how your team works best.

  • Clarity on each person's unique contribution
  • Identify gaps and overlaps in team function
  • Reduce frustration from mismatched roles
  • A shared language for how your team works best
